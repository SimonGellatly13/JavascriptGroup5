Sugar Cookies

Everyone needs an Easy Sugar Cookies Recipe! These are literally melt-in-your-mouth delicious.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder (aluminum-free)
  • 1/4 tsp salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C) with the rack in the center. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Using a stand mixer or handheld mixer, beat the butter and sugar together until creamy. Add the vanilla extract and egg, and beat to combine.
  3. Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture in 3 parts, mixing until fully incorporated.
  4. Divide the dough into two equal parts. On a lightly floured surface, roll each portion to 1/4-inch thickness. Use cookie cutters to cut out desired shapes.
  5. Place cookies on a parchment-lined or silicone-lined baking sheet and bake at 350°F for about 10 minutes, or until the edges just begin to turn golden.
  6. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before decorating.
