Strawberry Tart

This Tarte aux Fraises features a cookie crust filled with luscious vanilla pastry cream topped with fresh strawberries. A classic French dessert perfect for strawberry season!

INGREDIENTS

    PASTRY CREAM:
  • ½ vanilla bean
  • 6 tablespoons sugar, divided
  • 1 ⅓ cups milk, divided (330 ml total)
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon butter
    • ASSEMBLY:
  • 9-inch baked pâte sucrée or pâte sablée tart crust (or pie crust)
  • 1 pint strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry or apricot jam

INSTRUCTIONS

    PASTRY CREAM:
  1. Start the pastry cream by slitting open the vanilla bean with a small paring knife.
  2. Scrape out the vanilla bean seeds and add them to 3 tablespoons of sugar. Use your fingers to break up the seeds and combine with the sugar.
  3. Place the vanilla sugar, empty vanilla bean pod, and 1 cup of milk into a saucepan. Heat over medium, whisking to dissolve the sugar.
  4. Meanwhile, place the egg yolks in a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment and set to medium speed.
  5. Slowly add the remaining 3 tablespoons of sugar while mixing, then increase to high speed and whip until the mixture reaches ribbon stage, about 3–4 minutes. Scrape the bowl as needed.
  6. Whisk the cornstarch into the remaining ⅓ cup of milk until smooth and set aside.
  7. When the milk in the saucepan reaches a low boil, add the cold milk–cornstarch mixture to the stand mixer and mix on low speed.
  8. With the mixer still on low, slowly pour in the hot milk from the saucepan.
  9. Once combined, turn off the mixer and pour the entire mixture back into the saucepan.
  10. Cook over low heat, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens and begins to boil again, about 2 minutes.
  11. Whisk for an additional 2 minutes to remove any raw cornstarch taste. Whisk vigorously if the mixture looks lumpy.
  12. Whisk in the butter, then remove from heat and discard the vanilla bean pod.
  13. Scrape the pastry cream into a bowl and press plastic wrap directly onto the surface.
  14. Cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until cold.
    15. ASSEMBLY:
  15. Remove the outer ring from the tart pan and place the pâte sucrée shell onto a serving dish or cake stand.
  16. Whisk the chilled pastry cream until smooth and lump-free.
  17. Spread the pastry cream evenly across the base of the tart using an offset spatula.
  18. Cut the tops off the strawberries and slice each berry vertically into 3–4 thin slices.
  19. Arrange the strawberry slices over the pastry cream, starting from the outer edge and overlapping as you work toward the center.
  20. In a small bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of strawberry or apricot jam with 1 tablespoon of water.
  21. Microwave for about 10 seconds until melted and smooth.
  22. Brush the melted jam mixture over the strawberries to give them a shiny glaze.
  23. Chill the tart until ready to serve.
