Apple Tart

Sweet, refreshing, and perfect for any time of the week.

INGREDIENTS

    FILLING
  • 5 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/3 cup (71 g.) packed light brown sugar
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of kosher salt
    • CRUST
  • 1 1/3 cups (160 g.) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (53 g.) packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 10 Tbsp. unsalted butter, melted
    • FINISHING
  • 1 Tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 2 Tbsp. unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • Melted apricot preserves, for serving

INSTRUCTIONS

    FILLING:
  1. Arrange a rack in center of oven; preheat to 350°. In a large bowl, toss apples, brown sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt.
    2. CRUST:
  2. In a large bowl, whisk flour, brown sugar, salt, and cinnamon. Add butter and stir until a dough forms.
    Press crust mixture into bottom and up sides of a 10” or 11” tart pan with a removable bottom.
    3. ASSEMBLY:
  3. Arrange apples in bunches in crust, tightly packing. Sprinkle with granulated sugar, and dot top with butter.
    Bake tart until crust is golden brown and apples are tender, about 1 hour.
    Brush with apricot preserves. Let cool slightly.
