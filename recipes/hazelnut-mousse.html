Hazelnut Mousse

Sweet, refreshing, and perfect for any time of the week.

INGREDIENTS

  • 420 g cream
  • 90 g pasteurised egg yolk
  • 90 g sugar
  • 30 g water (1)
  • 6 g gelatine
  • 30 g water (2)
  • 168 g milk
  • 60 g hazelnut praliné 65%
  • 220 g Intense 35 Belgian milk couverture chocolate

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix the gelatine with cold water (1).
  2. Heat the sugar with water (2) to 120°C and combine it with the pasteurised egg yolk to create a pâte à bombe.
  3. Whisk the pâte à bombe until light and airy using a blender and whisk.
  4. Whisk the cream until soft peaks form using a blender and whisk.
  5. Boil the milk and dissolve the hydrated gelatine in it.
  6. Pour the hot milk over the chocolate and add the praliné.
  7. Mix with a stick blender until completely smooth.
  8. Use a spatula to fold the light pâte à bombe into the chocolate mixture, then gently fold in the whipped cream.
  9. Mix until fully combined.
  10. Use the praliné mousse immediately.
