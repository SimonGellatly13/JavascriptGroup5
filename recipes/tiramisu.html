Tiramisu Cake

Creamy, delicious and unbelievably EASY tiramisu recipe made with coffee soaked lady fingers, sweet and creamy mascarpone, and cocoa powder dusted on top.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream (360 ml)
  • 8 ounces (225 g) mascarpone cheese, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar (67 g)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (5 ml)
  • 1 1/2 cups cold espresso, prepared (360 ml)
  • 3 Tablespoons coffee-flavored liqueur (Kahlua or DaVinci), optional (45 ml)
  • 1 package Ladyfingers (Savoiardi brand)
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Add the whipping cream to a mixing bowl and beat on medium speed using electric mixers (or a stand mixer). Slowly add the sugar and vanilla, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
  2. Add the mascarpone cheese and gently fold until fully combined. Set aside.
  3. Add the coffee and liqueur to a shallow bowl.
  4. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture—do not soak, just lightly coat both sides—and arrange them in a single layer on the bottom of an 8×8-inch (or similar) pan.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers.
  7. Spread the remaining mascarpone mixture over the top.
  8. Generously dust the top with cocoa powder (a fine mesh strainer works best).
  9. Refrigerate for at least 3–4 hours, or overnight, before serving.
